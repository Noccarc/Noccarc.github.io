H210

High Flow Oxygen Therapy Device

About H210


Noccarc H210 provides respiratory support to spontaneously breathing patients by delivering high flow warmed and humidified gases through a nasal interface. The inbuilt turbine and electronic FiO2 control makes Noccarc H210 extremely easy to use, while providing maximum comfort to patients with acute to chronic breathing problems.
